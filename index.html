Overview

This page explains how to properly use the Whole Foods Market logo. It was made as a student project for learning how to use multiple CSS frameworks together.

Approved Logos

These are the versions of the logo that should be used on all materials.

  • Full-color logo on white or light backgrounds
  • White logo on dark backgrounds
  • Black logo for single-color print materials

Logo Spacing

Always leave enough white space around the logo. The space should be equal to at least the height of the "W" in "Whole".

Incorrect Uses

  • Do not stretch or distort the logo.
  • Do not change the logo color to anything not approved.
  • Do not place the logo over busy images.
