Logo Overview

The Whole Foods Market logo represents freshness, health, and sustainability. Below are the approved versions and usage rules for the logo.

Approved Logo Versions

  • Use official logos only — never redraw or edit.
  • Keep clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "W".
  • Do not distort, recolor, or add effects.

Incorrect Usage

Never change the logo color, stretch it, or overlay it on distracting backgrounds.
