Logo Overview

The Whole Foods Market logo represents freshness, sustainability, and trust. This guide explains how to use the logo correctly in digital and print materials.

Approved Logo Versions

  • Use official logos only — never redraw or edit.
  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the “W”.
  • Never distort, recolor, or add effects.

Incorrect Usage

Do not stretch, recolor, or place the logo over complex backgrounds.
