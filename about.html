Purpose

This website was created as a simple student project to explain the proper use of the Whole Foods Market logo. The goal is to show how brand consistency helps build recognition and trust.

Design Philosophy

Whole Foods’ design style emphasizes natural colors, clean typography, and balance. These guidelines help ensure the logo always reflects those values, whether used online or in print.
